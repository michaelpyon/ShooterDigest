PC competitive shooter market — Steam concurrent players, developer updates & community pulse

Week of March 09, 2026

Executive Summary

  • Split market: 4 gaining, 4 declining. Counter-Strike 2 leads at 1,506,156 Steam concurrent.
  • Notable: Apex Legends x Gundam Event (Apex Legends)
  • Notable: Marvel Rivals Version 20260305 Patch Notes (Marvel Rivals)
  • Steepest decline: Marvel Rivals -8.4% MoM. Now at 14% of ATH — late-stage decline.
  • Combined 24h Steam peaks: up 38.2% vs. prior period. Market-wide tailwind.
  • Content cadence is the differentiator: Rainbow Six Siege, Halo: MCC growing while Marvel Rivals, Destiny 2 (no active content) shrink.

Split market: 4 titles gaining, 4 declining. Counter-Strike 2 holds the top spot at 1,506,156 Steam concurrent players (83% of its all-time peak). The breadth of decline (Marvel Rivals, Destiny 2, Arc Raiders) points to a structural problem: studios that launched strong but haven't maintained content cadence are paying for it in retention. Watch Marvel Rivals and Rainbow Six Siege before the next digest: both have content drops signaled that could move the numbers.
